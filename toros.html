UX Development for Enerser: Fueling a Seamless Customer Experience

Case Study

Overview

In my role as a UX HMI Designer for Enerser, a prominent gas and oil company with fuel pumps across Mexico, I spearheaded the design and development of multiple digital and on-site user experiences. These initiatives aimed to modernize Enerser’s customer interactions through intuitive interfaces and convenient digital solutions, enhancing the overall user journey.

Role and Responsibilities

Primary Scope: Designed and delivered three core projects: Fuel Pump Interface Redesign: Revamped the fuel pump interface to ensure usability and accessibility, streamlining customer interactions at the pump. Mobile Payment App: Created an app enabling customers to pay for fuel directly from their smartphones, offering a seamless, cashless experience. Convenience Store Ordering App: Designed an app allowing users to purchase items from the adjacent convenience store while refueling, improving operational efficiency and customer convenience.

Challenges and Solutions

Challenge: Simplifying the fuel pump interface to accommodate diverse user demographics. Solution: Performed user testing to identify pain points in the existing interface. The redesigned interface featured clear visual hierarchies, simplified navigation, and large, accessible buttons to accommodate all users effectively. Challenge: Creating a seamless mobile payment experience to encourage digital adoption. Solution: Designed a step-by-step payment flow with minimal input requirements, integrating secure payment methods and real-time transaction feedback. Challenge: Enabling cross-platform convenience store ordering for busy customers. Solution: Designed an app with a clean, categorized product listing, integrated payment options, and notifications for order readiness to ensure a hassle-free experience.

Outcome and Impact

Streamlined Fuel Transactions: The redesigned fuel pump interface reduced transaction times and enhanced customer satisfaction. Increased Digital Adoption: The mobile payment app simplified fuel purchases, leading to higher adoption rates of digital payment methods. Enhanced Customer Convenience: The convenience store app improved order accuracy and efficiency, boosting overall customer satisfaction.

Lessons Learned

This project underscored the importance of combining digital design with real-world usability testing, particularly in ergonomic contexts. Balancing user-centered design with operational efficiency was crucial in delivering impactful solutions that transformed Enerser’s customer experience.
