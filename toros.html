Toros

UX Full Stack
UX Development for Toros Professional Baseball Team

Case Study

Overview

In 2022, as a UX Full Stack Designer for Toros, a professional baseball team in the Mexican Baseball League (LMB), I led the design and development of three major digital products. These included a ticket-purchasing website, a fan-focused iOS app with ticketing capabilities, and an in-house software application for tracking game and player statistics to improve team performance. My role spanned the entire UX lifecycle, from research and user testing to UI design and design system creation, ensuring a cohesive and impactful user experience across all platforms.

Role and Responsibilities

Primary Scope: Designed and developed a suite of applications to meet the needs of both fans and the internal team. The projects included: Ticket-Purchasing Website for fans, enabling easy access to tickets and providing a seamless online purchasing experience. iOS App for fans that integrated ticket purchasing and featured exclusive content, game schedules, and updates. In-House Statistics App for tracking player and game performance data, designed to assist the coaching staff in making data-driven decisions. Full Stack UX Involvement: Conducted UX research, user testing, and iterative design for all applications. Led the creation of a unified design system to ensure brand consistency across each product, streamlining design and development processes. Cross-functional Collaboration: Worked closely with developers, marketing, and operations teams to integrate functionality specific to fans’ and coaches' needs, providing a cohesive experience across platforms.

Challenges and Solutions

Challenge: Designing a seamless ticket-purchasing experience across web and mobile for varying user preferences. Solution: Conducted user research to identify key pain points in existing ticket-purchasing processes. Based on findings, developed an intuitive interface that minimized steps, simplified payment options, and provided clear seating visuals. Tested the design with a sample group to ensure ease of use before launch. Challenge: Creating an engaging fan experience through the iOS app to enhance fan loyalty. Solution: Focused on UX research to identify features that fans valued, such as real-time game updates, exclusive team content, and ticket purchasing. Iteratively designed the interface to prioritize these elements, ensuring fans had easy access to game schedules, exclusive updates, and promotional offers. Challenge: Developing a functional and user-friendly statistics application tailored to the coaching team’s needs. Solution: Conducted interviews and usability tests with coaches to identify key metrics and usability requirements. Designed a streamlined dashboard that enabled coaches to track players’ performance metrics efficiently, making the data easily accessible during games and post-game analysis.

Outcome and Impact

Enhanced User Engagement: The fan-focused iOS app boosted user engagement, offering a tailored digital experience that kept fans connected to the team with game updates, schedules, and ticketing. Streamlined Ticket Purchase Process: The ticket-purchasing website provided a simplified and efficient purchasing flow, leading to increased online sales and a positive user experience. Improved Team Performance Analysis: The in-house statistics application provided coaches with real-time, accessible data, helping them make informed decisions to enhance player performance and game strategies.

Lessons Learned

This project highlighted the value of understanding the distinct needs of different user groups—in this case, fans and coaching staff—and adapting UX design to meet those needs effectively. Developing a consistent design system across platforms ensured efficiency and brand coherence, proving essential in delivering a seamless experience to fans and operational staff alike. This case study demonstrates how a Full Stack UX approach can enhance user satisfaction, streamline processes, and deliver value to both fans and internal teams through thoughtful, end-to-end UX design.
